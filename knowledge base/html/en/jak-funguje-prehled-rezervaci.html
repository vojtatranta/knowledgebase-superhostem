Reservation management and calendar

How the booking overview works

On the Reservations page, you'll see all reservations in one place and can filter them by date, status, or property.

Help Center Library Next article →

Where you can find an overview of reservations

In the left menu, click on Reservation. A page with a subtitle will open Overview of all bookings from Airbnb, Booking.com and manual.

What is available on the site

  • Sync everything
  • New reservation
  • List
  • Calendar
  • View: Upcoming / Past
  • Status: All
  • Property: All
  • From the date
  • By date

What you will see in empty state

If you don't have any data yet, the page will display text You have no reservations yet and recommendations to connect calendars from Airbnb and Booking.com or add a reservation manually.

What to do if the overview is empty

  1. verify that you already have at least one property created
  2. connect the reservation source
  3. use if applicable New reservation
  4. click on Sync everything
