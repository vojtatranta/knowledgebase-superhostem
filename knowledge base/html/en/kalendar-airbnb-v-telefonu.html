Properties and calendars

To a calendar app

Add reservations from Superhostem to Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, or another iCal-compatible app. Use it for your own overview and calendar notifications.

Help Center Previous article Next article →
What it is good for

You see reservations in the calendar app you already use. For sharing with external people, the web calendar link is often better because access can be revoked.

When to use a phone calendar

Use the iCal export when you want reservations in your own calendar app. It is useful for a personal overview, a team calendar, or quick date checks without opening Superhostem.

  • you see reservations next to your personal calendar
  • you can use Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, or any app that supports iCal
  • you do not need to install another special app
  • you can set your own event alerts and notifications in the calendar app
If you need more control over who can see the calendar and how long access should work, use the web calendar link instead.
I want to share a web calendar linkBetter for cleaners, managers, or temporary external access.

What you need

Copy the iCal URL in Superhostem and paste it into your calendar app. The app then loads reservations automatically. If you change export settings, copy the new URL again.

Calendar apps refresh data on their own schedule, so changes may not appear immediately.
If guest name, guest count, price, or other details are missing in the calendar, Superhostem does not have that data yet. Enable reservation email forwarding to fill these details faster. Without it, the details must be entered manually by the guest or by you in the admin.
Fill details from emails automaticallyEmail forwarding loads reservation details without manual copying.

iPhone: Apple Calendar

  1. Copy the iCal URL in Superhostem.
  2. On iPhone, open Settings → Calendar → Accounts.
  3. Select Add Account → Other → Add Subscribed Calendar.
  4. Paste the URL, continue with Next, and save.
  5. In the Calendar app, set event alerts if needed.

Google Calendar and Android

In Google Calendar, add a calendar from URL in the desktop browser. The Android mobile app usually does not add a new iCal calendar directly.
  1. Copy the iCal URL in Superhostem.
  2. On a computer, open calendar.google.com.
  3. Next to Other calendars, click + and choose From URL.
  4. Paste the iCal URL and confirm with Add calendar.
  5. On Android, open Google Calendar with the same account and wait for sync.

What to watch out for

  • iCal is mainly for viewing, not editing reservations.
  • Updates in the external app can be delayed.
  • After changing export content, copy the new URL.
  • If you share the calendar with someone else, consider the web link because access can be revoked.
