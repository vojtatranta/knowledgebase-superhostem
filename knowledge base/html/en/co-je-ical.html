What is iCal

iCal is a format for sharing calendar data between different systems.

Help Center Library Next article →

Why is it important to Superhost

Thanks to the iCal connection, Superhost can retrieve information about booked and blocked dates from platforms such as Airbnb or Booking.com.

What you usually get with iCal

  • date of arrival
  • departure date
  • blocked dates

Which you don't usually get with iCal

  • detailed reservation price
  • fee breakdown
  • complete financial data
