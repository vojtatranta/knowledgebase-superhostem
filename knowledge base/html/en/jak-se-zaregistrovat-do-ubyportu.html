Legislation in the Czech Republic

How to register for Ubyport

If you are short-term accommodation of guests in the Czech Republic, you must also deal with reporting foreigners to the foreign police. A practical way is to register for Ubyport, where you will get access data for working with accommodated guests.

SuperhostemRequest web servicesA regular Ubyport account is not enough. Automation needs robotic data submission credentials.
SendingData mailbox is simplestSend to DS ybndqw9. Other options are signed email, post, or in-person filing.
WaitingAllow up to 30 daysThe police will send a protocol with login credentials after processing.
Important

You need two accesses: a regular accommodation-provider account and a robotic data submission account for Superhostem.

When you already have registration

A regular Ubyport login is not enough. For Superhostem, you need credentials for robotic or web-based data entry.

Data mailbox message template

Only add your IDUB. Then copy the full message body and paste it into the data mailbox.

Recipient

Subject

Hello,
I have a registered accommodation facility with IDUB: [your IDUB].

Please create an account for robotic data submission for superhostem.cz (Superhostem s.r.o.).

Thank you.
