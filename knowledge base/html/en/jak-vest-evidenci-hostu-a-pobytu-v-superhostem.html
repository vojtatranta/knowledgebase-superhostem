Automation and communication

How to keep records of guests and stays in Superhostem

In Superhost, domovní kniha follows up on reservations, guestbook and information that guests fill in before arrival. Thanks to this, you do not have to rewrite the data manually in a separate document and you have them linked to a specific stay.

Help Center Previous article Next article →
What awaits you next?

First, check that you have the guestbook and automation set up correctly, then you verify the filled-in data for reservations and the resulting guest records.

What must work beforehand

Booking in the system

domovní kniha in Superhost stands on correctly loaded reservations and their details.

Guestbook or guest form

You need a way to collect data from guests before they arrive or at check-in.

Communication set up

Automation needs to send guests the right link at the right time so they can fill out the information.

Where to start in Superhost

You practically start with reservations and follow-up communication with the guest. In the booking you can see if the guest has filled in the necessary information, and you can follow up on the guestbook or form, which was sent to him.

  • check that the reservation in the system matches the actual stays
  • verify that the guest received the correct link to the form or guestbook
  • when making a reservation, make sure that the filled-in information is complete
  • you can generate guest and stay records at any time after selecting a period from–to

How it works in practice

Send the guest a link via automation

The guest will receive a link to the form or guestbook before arrival. This is where data collection for guest registration begins.

The guest completes the details of the stay

Once the guest fills out the form, the data is linked to a specific reservation. You don't have to look for them sideways outside the system.

Check the reservation and completeness of the data

For a specific reservation, verify that the data you need for guest registration and other related obligations is not missing. You can then generate an overview for the selected period by date at any time from–to.

Work with records continuously, not retrospectively

The biggest advantage of Superhost is that you collect data continuously during normal work with reservations. This eliminates later manual rewriting into a separate domovní kniha.

What to look out for

  • the guest really got the right link to the form or guestbook
  • the reservation is correctly assigned to a specific property in the system
  • the stay includes the data you need for guest registration
  • the automation leaves in time so that the guest has time to complete the data before arrival
