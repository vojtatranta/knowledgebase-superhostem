# Superhostem.cz — Project Definition (PDE)

## Project Overview

Superhostem.cz is a platform designed to simplify and automate **operations, financial visibility, and regulatory administration for short-term rental hosts** in the Czech Republic.

The system acts as a **central operating dashboard for hosts**, combining reservation tracking, revenue visibility, and administrative support into one platform.

The long-term vision is to build the **core operating system for Czech short-term rental hosts**, removing operational complexity and reducing manual work.

Initial focus is **the Czech Republic market**.

European expansion is considered a **long-term future opportunity**, not a short-term goal.

---

# Core Product Concept

Short-term rental hosts typically manage their operations using multiple disconnected tools:

* Airbnb / Booking dashboards
* email notifications
* spreadsheets
* manual revenue tracking
* accounting tools
* regulatory reporting systems

This creates operational complexity and limited financial visibility.

Superhostem.cz centralizes these workflows into a **single operational platform**.

Hosts gain visibility into:

* reservations
* revenues
* property performance
* operational overview
* regulatory obligations

---

# Core Data Architecture

The platform collects booking and financial data using two primary sources.

## 1. iCal Reservation Integration

Hosts connect booking platforms through **iCal feeds**.

Typical sources include:

* Airbnb
* Booking.com
* other OTA platforms

iCal feeds provide:

* reservation start and end dates
* blocked calendar periods
* booking identifiers

However, iCal feeds **do not contain financial information**.

---

## 2. Email Forwarding for Revenue Enrichment

To obtain financial information, the system supports **forwarding booking emails**.

Hosts forward booking notifications from platforms such as Airbnb to a system email address.

Email parsing extracts:

* booking price
* platform fees
* guest information
* booking identifiers

This allows the system to reconstruct:

* revenue per reservation
* revenue per property
* occupancy analytics
* financial summaries

By combining **iCal data and parsed email data**, the system can generate financial insights **without requiring official OTA API integrations**.

# Ambassador Growth Model

To accelerate growth, Superhostem.cz will use an **ambassador-based distribution strategy**.

Ambassadors are individuals or companies who introduce hosts to the platform and help onboard them.

Typical ambassadors include:

* experienced Airbnb hosts
* co-hosts managing multiple apartments
* short-term rental consultants
* property managers
* host community leaders

Ambassadors receive **revenue share from subscription revenue generated through their referrals**.

This model enables growth through **trusted relationships in host communities**.

---

# Ambassador Commission Structure

Commission applies to **subscriptions generated by the ambassador**.

Higher commissions apply to **new sales**, while **renewals have lower commissions**.

# Renewal Commission

Subscription renewals have **lower commission percentages** than new sales.

Example structure:

Initial sale
20–40% depending on tier

Renewal commission
10–20% depending on tier

Renewal commissions reward ambassadors for bringing customers who remain active on the platform.

# Market Focus — Czech Republic

The initial market is the **Czech short-term rental ecosystem**.

Most listings are concentrated in:

* Prague
* Brno
* tourist regions

The primary target users are:

* multi-property hosts
* professional co-hosts
* small property managers

These users experience the highest operational complexity and therefore benefit the most from automation tools.
